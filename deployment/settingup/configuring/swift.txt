======================
Swift : Asset Storage
======================

The *Asset Store* provides a solution to store, maintain, and version the physical media files (viz; Images, InDesign documents, PDFs, etc).

* Asset Storage requires One Account.

* Asset Storage would need 5 Containers:


    1.	Attachments
    2.	Icons
    3.	Images
    4.	Documents
    5.	Videos
