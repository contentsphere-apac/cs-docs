Define Article-Asset Relationship in an Onboarding FileÂ¶

  • We define relationship between articles and assets in Article-Asset Relationship sheet. We define which assets are used for an article in this sheet.
  • We defined all articles in an Article sheet and assets in Asset sheet. In Article-Asset Relationship sheet, we will take the article and asset reference from Article and Asset sheets respectively.

This sheet will contain the following details column-wise:

Details Description
Source You need to mention the unique identification of an article in this column. This will be the ArticleID defined in the Article sheet.
Destination You need to mention the unique identification of an asset that you want to associate with article. This will be the AssetID defined in the Asset sheet. Note: There can be multiple assets associated with a single article, so there can be multiple entries/ rows of different Destination for a single Source.
