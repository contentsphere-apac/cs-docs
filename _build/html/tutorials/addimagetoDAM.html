Add an Image to the DAM¶

Products with displayed images are more appealing for the users to the system, which can be stored in DAM (Data Asset Management).

How-to: Add an Image¶

To do this, follow these steps:

  1. Click All.


  1. Click DAM.


  1. Hover on the “+”.


  1. Then, click Image.


  1. Click the Add Image icon on the File Preview section, and select your desired image from your computer.


  1. The image appears on the File Preview section, and enter a name for the image.


  1. Then, click Save.


  1. Click Metadata.


  1. The available metadata on the Meta Data section will auto-complete but fields can be manually filled out as desired.
