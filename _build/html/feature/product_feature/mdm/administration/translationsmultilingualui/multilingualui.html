Multilingual UI¶

Multilingual UI (User Interface) is a feature that provides translations for the menu items in the UI so that the UI can be used in multiple languages.

How to do: Manage Multilingual UI¶

To do this, follow these steps:

  1. Go to PIM.


  1. Hover over the “+” icon.


  1. Click on Loose Bundle.


  1. Enter the necessary information.


  1. Click Add products.


  1. Click the green button to add the required products (button turns to blue when selected), and click Add All.


  1. To add more products, click Return, and repeat the previous steps.



  1. To add an image, click Relationships tab.


  1. Hover over on the “+” icon and click Add Entity.


  1. Hover over on the “+” icon and click Image.



  1. Click Add Image again.



  1. View bundle on the top section of the page.
