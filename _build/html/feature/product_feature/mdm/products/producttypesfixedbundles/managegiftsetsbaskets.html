Manage Gift Sets/Baskets¶

The Manage Gift Sets or Basket feature allows a user to group articles together in a specified quantity and sells them as a package to the customer.

Key Term:
  • Product Information Management (PIM): facilitates the central management and maintenance of an enterprise’s product data, content or any related information.
Description:
  • This feature allows the retailer to manage a Bundle of Single Articles. The advantage of a Bundle is in grouping complementary products for sale in a Gift Set of Basket.
Related topics:
  • Manage Displays
Application:

  • To create a bundle of products to form one product.

  • The bundle is sold as an individual product to the customer.

  • As a manufacturer/retailer the user can’t increase the number of quantities in the bundle.

  • Example:

    A new dog owner may purchase a ‘New Pet Dog Starter Kit’ which includes a bag of food, a collar and a small bed.

How-to: Manage Gift Sets/Baskets¶

To do manage gift sets/baskets, follow these steps:


  1. Go to PIM.


  1. Hover on “+” icon.


  1. Click on Fixed Bundle.


  1. Enter the required information.


  1. Click Add products to add the required products.


  1. Click the Green box to select the desired product (Green box turns to Blue when selected), and click Add All.


  1. Product added successfully. To add more products, click Return.


  1. To add an image, click Relationships tab.


  1. To add an image, click “+”.


  1. Click on the Black box of the desired image (Black turns to Blue when selected), and click Add All. The desired image can also be dragged to the right section.


  1. To view the added image, click Relationships tab to view the added image.


  1. To view the added product, click the General tab.
