Add New Users¶

How to do: Add New Users¶

To do this, follow these steps:


  1. Click Settings.


  1. Click Partner Admin.


  1. Click Users from the left sidebar.


  1. Then, click “+”.


  1. Enter data in the fields…and click CREATE.


  1. Enter a name for the image… and click SAVE.


Icons for the user can also be added to the created user. To do this, follow the steps below: |

  1. Click add icon button.


  1. View the added icon.
