Drill Down for KPI Reports¶

Drill Down for KPI Reports is a data governance feature that sets up drill down criteria for DQ (Data Quality) reports so that responsible users can explore the quality issues.

How to do: Drill Down for KPI Reports¶

To do this, follow these steps:

  1. Click Administration icon.


  1. Click the Data Governance tab from the top.


  1. Click KPIs.


  1. Click the + icon at the top right to create a new KPI.


  1. Enter a name and code(optional). Then, click CREATE to create the KPI.


  1. Once the KPI is created, it will appear in the list of KPI, click the Edit icon.


  1. On the KPI Information tab, click to select Class. then, Taxonomy.


  1. Click the Completeness tab. Then, click to select attributes and Tags.






  1. Click Apply. Click SAVE to save the changes.


  1. Repeat the similars teps for Conformity, Uniqueness and Accuracy tab. Click to select the attributes and tags.


  1. To assign the KPI to a role, click Partner Administration tab and select the Organization where the role is present ( in this demo, we selected “Fashionista”).




  1. Click Roles tab and select a role ( in this demo, we selected ” Garment Supplier”). Then, select a KPI and click APPLY.


  1. Logout from the system and login with the user belonging to the role, to view the KPI for that role.
