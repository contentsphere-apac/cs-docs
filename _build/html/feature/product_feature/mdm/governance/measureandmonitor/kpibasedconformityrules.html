KPI-based Conformity Rules¶

The KPI-based Conformity Rules is a data governance feature that sets up KPI-based Conformity Rules to assess the conformance of an Article to the acceptable standards.

Key Term:
  • Key Performance Indicator (KPI): is a measurement used to determine the quality of product data added into the system in relation to the set standard.
Description:
  • KPIs help to find the accuracy of the products on the basis of articles attributes taxonomy and tags.
  • KPIs check the following 4 factors: Completeness, Conformity, Uniqueness, Accuracy.
  • KPIs also drill down on the basis of Taxonomy and Tags.
  • The KPI: Conformity checks the accuracy of data in the product.
Related topics:
  • Tag Management features
  • Attribute Management features
  • Manage Taxonomy
  • Monitor Data Quality
Application:

  • Data completeness is one of the most important data quality and accuracy checking characteristics. It is the most important factor for KPIs.

  • Example:

    An organization sells furniture products. They have attributes for Length of the wood and tags for Color. Then, the Conformity Rule helps users to check that the length should not be more than 400m.

How to do: KPI-based Conformity Rules¶

To do this feature, follow these steps:


  1. Log in and go to Administration.


  1. Click on the Data Governance tab.


  1. Click on KPIs.


  1. Click on the “+” icon to create a new KPI.


  1. Fill out the name and click on the Create button.


  1. Once the KPI is created, the new KPI will be shown on the list. Click on the Edit button on the right.


  1. Select a Class and Taxonomy for which the KPI is designed to be used. Click the Apply button.


  1. In the sublevels, add Tags or Taxonomies, in which the KPI is to be categorized into.


  1. Click on the Conformity tab and click on the “+” icon to create a new rule.


  1. Provide the details for the Label, Attributes, and Tags to which the user would want to apply the rule. Select the rules according to the requirements.


  1. Click the Save button.
