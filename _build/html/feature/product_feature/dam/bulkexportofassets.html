Bulk Export of Assets¶

The Bulk Export of Assets feature is a DAM feature that allows exporting of digital assets in bulk. The digital assets that may be exported in DAM are the following:

Key Term:
  • DAM: Digital Asset Management offers a solution to enterprises to store, organize, find, and retrieve digital files. Digital files in the DAM are linked to the PIM through relationships.
Description:
  • This feature allows a user to download assets (image, document, video) in bulk.
Related Topic:
  • Bulk Import of Assets
  • Images
  • Documents
  • Videos
Application:

  • Example:

    Supplier A has about 50 images, 20 videos, and 30 documents to be downloaded. He can download everything at the same time by using the bulk download feature.

How to do: Bulk Export of Assets¶

To export assets in bulk, follow these steps:


  1. Log in and click on the Explore icon.


  1. Click on DAM from the selection on the left.


  1. Select the assets to be downloaded. Note that the selected assets are highlighted.


  1. Click on the Bulk Download button.


Note: Wait for the download to be completed; then, view the assets.
