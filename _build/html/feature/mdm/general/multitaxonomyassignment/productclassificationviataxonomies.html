CORE 60: Product Classification via Taxonomies¶

The Product Classification via Taxonomies feature provides the functionality to associate a product to multiple taxonomies.

Version: 1.0
User Story:
  • As a user, I want to associate a product to a taxonomy and then to another taxonomy to classify the product to multiple taxonomies so that I have a more refined product classification.
Description:
  • Taxonomies are structured hierarchies which can be used to classify the products in the system.
  • Products can have multiple taxonomies set by a user to have a more refined classification and easier search.
Prerequisite:
  • Articles created
  • Taxonomy
Application:

  • This feature is used to easily classify, as well as, to search for a product with the help of taxonomy.

  • Example:

    If a user has an energy drink as a product that is classified under the Energy Drinks taxonomy, the user can also associate the product to the Beverages taxonomy that will allow the product to be easily searched by using the taxonomies.

How to do: Product Classification via Taxonomies¶

To classify a product to multiple taxonomies, follow these steps:


  1. On the product page, click on the Add Taxonomy button. Notice that the product is already associated with the Energy Drinks taxonomy.


  1. Select a parent taxonomy from the drop-down menu (e.g. Products).



  1. To further classify the product, click on Classify Further button.


  1. Select a node from the list. Classify further until the desired taxonomy is achieved (e.g. Food > Beverages > Sports and Energy Drinks).




  1. Go to PIM and click on the Choose Taxonomies button.


  1. Choose the Products taxonomy, followed by the Food node and then the Beverages node. Click the Apply button.


  1. View the results that contain the products belonging to the selected taxonomies.
