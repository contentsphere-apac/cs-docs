CORE 67: Manage Products across Collections¶

Manage Products across Collections is a feature that allows users to copy or move products or objects between collections.

Version: 1.0
User Story:
  • As a user, I want to copy or move products or objects between collections.
Description:
  • Collections are created to collect and store a group of products based on the relevance of the user.
  • Collections can have their own hierarchy and the products in the collection can belong to these hierarchies.
  • Collections help to quickly classify a group of products.
Prerequisite:
  • Product created
  • Collection hierarchy
Application:

  • Collections help the user to group the products and place them in hierarchies.

  • Example:

    A collection can be made for a few furtniture products and in the collection, hierarchies can be made based on the class type for the article.

How to do: Manage Products across Collections¶

This How To guide provides instructions on how to copy or move products across collections. To do this, follow these steps:


  1. Go to PIM.


  1. Go to Collections and click on the Organize button.


  1. On the collections page, click on the Add New Collection button and enter the name of that new collection. This will add a new collection.


  1. Go back to the first collection and select the product you would like to copy or move.

    Note: Multiple products can be selected for copying.

  1. Once selected, click on the Copy button on the upper right side of the page.

  1. Go to the collection where you want to copy the product.

  1. Click Paste.

The product has been added successfully. Follow the same steps to move the products. To do that, click Cut instead of Copy.
