CORE 68: Collection Hierarchy¶

The Collection Hierarchy feature provides general users the ability to view their collections within a structured hierarchy of collections.

Version: 1.0
User Story: As a user, I want to organize and view my collections within a structured hierarchy of collections.
Description:
  • The Collection Hierarchy creates levels in the hierarchy for the products.
  • The products can be moved or copied between these levels.
Prerequisite: Collection that contains products
Application:
  • Hierarchies in collection hnelps to categorically structure the products or group of products.
  • The collection can cotnain any number of products and can be moved or copied between the levels of hierarchies.
  • Example:

A collection of furniture products having level based on product type such as office and home furniture, chairs, tables, and others.
How To A How to do: Collection Hierarchy document is available below.

How to do: Collection Hierarchy¶

This How To guide provides instructions on how to organize and view the collections within a structured hierarchy (of collections). To do this, follow these steps:

  1. Go to Collections and click on the Organize button.


  1. On the collections page, click on the Add New Collection button and enter the name of the new collection.


  1. Repeat the previous step to create a hierarchy of the product, accordingly.
