ADMIN 13: Define Relationships¶

The Define Relationships feature provides the functionality to set up relationships between entities.

Version: 1.0
User Story:
  • As an administrator, I want to set up relationships between Entities (e.g. Article to Market).
Description:
  • This feature can link two objects/entities in the system. An example of such link is the relationship of an Article to a Market or Market to a Promotion.
Prerequisite:
  • Article-to-Market relationship.
  • Article-to-Supplier relationship.
  • Market-to-Promotion relationship.
Application:
  • The Define Relationships will allow an administrator to link an article or a product to its target market, which is the location or a group of consumers that article/product is intended for.

How to do: Define Relationships¶

To do this feature, follow these steps:


  1. Click on the Settings button.


  1. Go to the Relationship Configuration tab.


  1. Create a new relationship by clicking the ‘+’ icon.




  1. Provide the name for the relationship and then, select the two desired entity classes to be linked (in Side1 and Side2) using the drop-down list. In the example shown, a relationship is created for Article and the Region articles.


  1. Provide the information for the Label and then, select the Cardinality of the bi-directional relationship. In cardinality, choose either Many or One depending on the relationship.


  1. Tick on the checkboxes for the elements to be editable. Select either one or both sides.


  1. Once done, click on the Save button.


  1. Once saved, view that the relationship is applied and ready.

Note: The Class and Cardinality will not be editable once the relationship is saved.
