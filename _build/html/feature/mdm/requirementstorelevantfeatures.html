Requirements to Features¶

Classification / Hierarchies¶

Requirement(s): Relevant Feature(s):
Ability to define multiple categories in a hierarchical structure. Feature Example
Ability to classify product into multiple hierarchy levels.
Ability to associate a product with more than one category.
Ability to reclassify product into new or additional categories.
Ability for categories to inherit attributes from top-level categories.
Ability to link product for the purposes of bundling, upselling, or cross-selling.
Ability to link product for the purposes of bundling, upselling, or cross-selling.
Ability to support multiple hierarchies.

Product Attributes¶

Requirement(s): Relevant Feature(s):
Ability to support product information version control and rollback capabilities.
Ability to define/delete/modify product attributes without IT involvement.
Ability to define common attributes across multiple categories.
Ability to support complex multi-value attributes.
Ability to support language and culture localization.
Ability to maintain product variants by size/color.

Publishing / Syndication¶

Requirement(s): Relevant Feature(s):
Ability to support time-based publishing and expiration of content.
Ability to export and syndicate product data to multiple sources in a near real-time basis.
Ability to export changes-only to multiple sources.
Ability to provide accelerators/connectors for vendor syndication networks.

Digital Asset Management¶

Requirement(s): Relevant Feature(s):
Ability to support multiple types of unstructured digital assets (images, video, audio, animation, documents).
Ability to manage images in low and high resolution.
Ability to manage metadata.
Ability to associate multiple digital assets with related products.

Product Management¶

Requirement(s): Relevant Feature(s):
Ability to bulk manage product attributes and content.
Ability to bulk copy selected product data (hierarchy, attributes, etc) from a master product.
Ability to define product content and attributes by channel (web, mobile, in-store showroom, store, customer service, etc).

Workflow¶

Requirement(s): Relevant Feature(s):
Ability to set up workflow that will allow proofing and approval of product catalog changes before deploying to specific environments.
Ability to define workflow that has both human and system steps.
Ability to configure data management to the product attribute and user/role level.
Ability for the system to provide delegation or reassignment of work between authorized users.
Ability for the system to generate pre-defined alerts.

Portals¶

Requirement(s): Relevant Feature(s):
Ability for system to offer integrated vendor onboarding to allow vendors to manage content.
Ability for vendors to bulk upload product information.
Ability for system to review vendor product content for completion/accuracy based on defined business rules by product category/type.
Ability for system to offer portal for internal access to PIM content.

Integration¶

Requirement(s): Relevant Feature(s):
Ability to provide an open API to all components to accommodate flexible application integration.
Ability to provide integration agents for access to legacy systems.

Auditing¶

Requirement(s): Relevant Feature(s):
Ability to maintain revisions and history tracking serving as an audit trail of previous versions of data.

Security¶

Requirement(s): Relevant Feature(s):
Ability to define access profiles with various roles and responsibilities.
Ability to restrict system access by user group or role.
Ability to support single sign-on.
Ability to support full-text search of content, metadata, and attributes.
Ability to search by predefined search parameters.
