Key Terms:
===========

* PIM
   Product Information Management (PIM) facilitates the central management and maintenance of an enterprise’s product data, content or any related information.

* PEP
   Partner Engagement Platform (PEP) is a portal which allows a Contentserv customer’s partners to engage with their system.
   Deployed for supplier engagement, collaboration, product onboarding, simple PIM and product data onboarding/offboarding.
